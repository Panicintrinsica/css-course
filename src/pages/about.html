Our History: From Museum Halls to Police Headquarters

The Raccoon City Police Department has a long and proud history of service. Established to meet the needs of a growing community, the department has evolved over the decades, always maintaining its core commitment to the safety and security of Raccoon City's residents. In 1969, recognizing the need for a more centralized and spacious facility, the city government acquired the former Raccoon City Museum building to serve as the new police headquarters.

While the artworks were relocated, the building's distinctive architecture and prominent location in the heart of the city remain. This unique facility provides the RPD with ample space for operations, training, and administration, allowing us to serve the public more effectively. Its history as a cultural institution is embraced as part of the city's unique character, repurposed to house those dedicated to protecting its citizens.

Our Commitment: Dedication to the Citizens of Raccoon City

The Raccoon City Police Department operates under a set of core values that guide every officer and staff member:

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest ethical standards in all actions.
  • Professionalism: Performing duties with competence, diligence, and respect.
  • Service: Placing the needs of the community first and responding effectively.
  • Courage: Facing challenges and danger with resolve to protect others.

We are committed to providing the highest level of police service possible. This includes continuous training for our officers and staff, ensuring they are equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to handle the diverse challenges of modern policing. Furthermore, the department actively seeks to utilize up-to-date technology and resources to enhance our operational effectiveness and meet the public safety needs of Raccoon City.

Department Organization

  • Patrol Division: Responsible for uniformed patrol, emergency response, and initial incident handling.
  • Detective Bureau: Tasked with investigating serious crimes and preparing cases for prosecution.
  • Support Services: Providing essential administrative, technical, and logistical support.
  • Special Tactics and Rescue Service (S.T.A.R.S.): An elite unit handling high-risk situations and specialized rescue operations.
