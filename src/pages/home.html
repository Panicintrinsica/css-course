A Message from Cheif Brian Irons

Welcome to the official website of the Raccoon City Police Department. As Chief of Police, it is my distinct honor to lead the dedicated men and women who serve and protect this remarkable community. Here at the RPD, we are deeply committed to fostering a strong partnership with our citizens, working hand-in-hand to ensure Raccoon City remains a safe and prosperous place to live, work, and raise a family.

Our department takes immense pride in its professionalism and its ability to adapt to the evolving needs of our growing and vibrant city. We strive for excellence in every aspect of our duties, from community patrol to complex investigations. This commitment is reflected not only in our personnel but also in our unique headquarters, a historic city landmark repurposed to serve the cause of public safety. Its central location and character stand as a symbol of Raccoon City's heritage and our dedication to serving its future. We invite you to explore this website to learn more about our services, initiatives, and how we can work together for a safer Raccoon City. The presence of this online resource itself reflects the city's forward-thinking approach and commitment to utilizing modern tools for community engagement, an early adoption compared to some municipalities. This embrace of new communication methods is part of ensuring the RPD remains responsive and accessible.

RPD Mission Statement

The Raccoon City Police Department is dedicated to protecting the lives and property of all citizens through fair, professional, and responsive policing. We strive to build partnerships with the community to prevent crime and enhance the quality of life in Raccoon City. This mission guides our daily operations and strategic planning, ensuring our focus remains steadfastly on public safety and community well-being, aligning with established principles of effective law enforcement.

Community Program Spotlight: Neighborhood Watch

The RPD strongly believes that effective crime prevention requires a partnership between the police and the community we serve. Our Neighborhood Watch program empowers citizens to become active participants in maintaining safety in their own areas. By working together, neighbors can significantly reduce opportunities for crime and build stronger, more connected communities. Learn how you can start or join a Neighborhood Watch group on our Community Safety page. This initiative is a cornerstone of our community policing philosophy, a widely recognized approach to enhancing public safety through collaboration
