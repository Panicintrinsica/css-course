Patrol Division: The Front Line of Service

The Patrol Division forms the backbone of the Raccoon City Police Department. Uniformed officers are typically the first point of contact between the RPD and the public. They respond to a wide range of calls for service, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Key responsibilities include:

  • Responding to emergency (9-1-1) and non-emergency calls.
  • Conducting visible patrols throughout the city's neighborhoods and business districts to deter crime.
  • Enforcing traffic laws and investigating traffic accidents.
  • Conducting preliminary investigations at crime scenes.
  • Providing assistance and information to the public.

Patrol officers play a crucial role in community interaction, building relationships, and addressing local concerns. Their constant presence provides the foundation for public safety in Raccoon City, handling the bulk of day-to-day police work and establishing the baseline of standard operations.

When serious crimes occur, the RPD's Detective Bureau takes the lead in the investigation. Comprised of experienced investigators, the Bureau works diligently to identify suspects, gather evidence, and build strong cases for prosecution. Their duties involve:

  • Conducting in-depth investigations into major crimes such as homicide, robbery, assault, burglary, and fraud.
  • Interviewing victims, witnesses, and suspects.
  • Collecting and analyzing physical evidence.
  • Preparing detailed reports and testifying in court.

Within the Bureau, detectives may develop specialized expertise in areas like Property Crimes or Crimes Against Persons, ensuring thorough and knowledgeable investigation of complex cases.

Special Tactics and Rescue Service (S.T.A.R.S.): Raccoon City's Elite Response Team

Established in 1996, the Special Tactics and Rescue Service (S.T.A.R.S.) represents the Raccoon City Police Department's commitment to providing the highest level of response capability for critical incidents. This elite unit was formed as part of the "Bright Raccoon 21" city development initiative, reflecting a proactive approach to ensuring public safety in the face of potentially complex and high-risk situations that can arise in a developing urban environment. The unit's formation, supported through civic partnerships, ensures the RPD is prepared for diverse challenges

S.T.A.R.S. is composed of highly trained officers selected for their exceptional skills and judgment. The unit is equipped with specialized tools and resources to handle situations beyond the scope of standard patrol or detective operations. Their responsibilities include

  • Responding to high-risk incidents such as hostage situations, barricaded suspects, and active threats.
  • Executing high-risk warrants and arrests.
  • Conducting specialized search and rescue operations in challenging environments.
  • Providing tactical support to other RPD divisions.
  • Maintaining readiness for potential counter-terrorism scenarios, an increasing consideration for law enforcement agencies during this period.

Organized into Alpha and Bravo teams for operational flexibility, S.T.A.R.S. personnel undergo rigorous, continuous training to maintain peak readiness. Their existence provides Raccoon City with a crucial asset for resolving the most dangerous and demanding situations, ensuring the safety of both citizens and officers.
